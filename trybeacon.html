Network segmentation is a core network protection strategy

Critical infrastructure and ICS/OT security policies require segmentation e.g., ISO/IEC ISO27002:2022 Control 8.22 - Segregation of Networks. In addition, under the NIS2 Directive (Directive (EU) 2022/2555), network segmentation and segregation are critical, non-negotiable risk management measures required for essential and important entities to bolster cyber resilience.

Frequently asked questions

Is the trial really free?

Yes. The 14-day trial does not require a credit card and provides full access to the necessary tools for testing isolation.

Is Beacon safe for OT environments?

Beacon is designed to be lightweight and non-intrusive. It does not rely on aggressive scanning or high-bandwidth activity, making it safe for sensitive PLC and industrial controller environments.

What happens if Beacon reaches home?

If a Beacon reaches home from an isolated segment, it means the segment has a path out. Beacon Home will provide technical details about the escape path (e.g., protocol used) to help you investigate the root cause.

How long does deployment take?

Your first Beacon can typically be deployed in minutes. Depending on your choice (VM, Docker, etc.), it's usually just a matter of launching the image and verifying connectivity to Beacon Home.

Do I need to install agents everywhere?

No. The trial is designed around selected Beacon deployments in the specific segments you want to verify. You don't need agents on every endpoint.
